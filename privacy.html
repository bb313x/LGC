Your Privacy Comes First

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you contact us or engage our services, we may collect:

  • Law firm name, contact information, and practice details
  • Attorney names and professional credentials
  • Email, phone, and business address
  • Practice areas, case volume, and marketing objectives
  • Payment information (processed securely through third parties)
  • IP address, browser data, and site usage analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Develop and deliver tailored legal marketing strategies
  • Generate and nurture qualified client leads
  • Provide campaign management and reporting
  • Send service updates and performance reports
  • Improve our platform and user experience
  • Comply with applicable legal requirements

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or trade your information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers (payment processing, hosting, analytics, advertising platforms)
  • Courts or regulatory bodies if legally required
  • Professional advisors for compliance or legal matters

All third parties are bound by strict data protection agreements.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tools to enhance your experience, remember preferences, and measure campaign performance. You can manage cookies through your browser settings, though this may limit site functionality.

5. Your Privacy Rights (NJ & Federal)

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of certain data uses
  • Receive your data in portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@letsgrowclients.com or 732-484-3000.

6. Data Security

We use encryption, secure servers, firewalls, and strict access controls to protect your information. We follow industry best practices for data security. However, we encourage you to use strong passwords and report any suspected breaches immediately.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our services are designed for law firm professionals, not children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover a child has submitted information, we will delete it promptly. Parents or guardians may request removal by contacting us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as needed. Changes will be posted with an updated effective date, and your continued use indicates acceptance.

9. Contact Us
