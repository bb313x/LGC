Our Story

Founded on Real Legal Industry Experience

Lets Grow Clients was born from frustration. Our co-founders saw brilliant attorneys struggling to fill their pipelines, not because their work wasn't excellent, but because they didn't have a partner who truly understood legal marketing.

Bryan brought strategic vision and deep expertise in AI and technology adoption across industries. Kirk brought unmatched sales and marketing acumen, along with a philosophy of keeping everything simple and results-focused.

Together, they built a firm specifically designed for law practices — understanding everything from PIP and no-fault claims to personal injury, criminal defense, and family law dynamics. Every strategy we deploy is informed by the realities of running a legal practice.
